About Me

Native of Atlanta. Explorer of New York. And now calling Chattanooga home.


Jasmine M. Clark is the friendly face behind JMC Marketing. After graduating NYU Stern School of Business, she joined Unilever as a member of their Future Leaders Program, a fast track to management rotational program. While there, she led various projects in sales and brand development.

During her junior year in college, she discovered her passion for small businesses and start-ups while working with Columbia University engineers to launch an affordable green construction business model and app.

Her idea to birth JMC Marketing in 2013 was simple. More start-ups, small businesses, and small-to-mid-size creative agencies needed access to quality strategic consulting within their budgetary means. Her mantra while working on different projects: passion + strategic planning wins every time.
