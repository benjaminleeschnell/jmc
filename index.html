About Me

Native of Atlanta. Explorer of New York. And now calling Chattanooga home.

Jasmine M. Clark is the friendly face behind JMC Marketing. After graduating NYU Stern School of Business, she then joined Unilever as a future leader program (competitive rotational program to 3-year trek to management) member where she led projects in sales and brand development. She discovered her passion for small businesses and start-ups her junior year in college as she joined Columbia University engineers as the business & marketing mind to launch a business model for affordable green construction.

Her idea to birth JMC Marketing in 2013 was simple. More start-ups, small businesses, and small-to-mid-size creative agencies needed access to quality strategic consulting within their budgetary means.
